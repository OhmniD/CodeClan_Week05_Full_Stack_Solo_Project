Formula 1 Results App


This app is for keeping track of both the team and driver Formula One world championships. Points for both the driver and team standings are automatically calculated.


  • -The top three for each championship is shown below, click through to see the full standings.

  • -You can view a driver's profiles and results by clicking the Drivers button on the top navigation bar.

  • -To view and edit team information, click the Teams button.

  • -To view and edit the rounds on the F1 calendar, or view the results for a specific track, click the Rounds button.

  • -To add results for a round, click the Add Race Results button. Be careful to make sure you only enter a driver once in the results.
